Maximizing Mental Funtioning

hmm im thinkin abt how when it comes to maximizing ur mental functioning/capacity/receptivity/pleasure in some area , i think its not abt either simple increased exposure, or simple decreased exposure :o

the pieces being correlated here are

  • mental capacity (what ur brain Can do)
    • increase
    • or decrease
  • willingful engagement (what u choose to engage in)
    • increase
    • decrease

some common fragments of arguments ppl make and thoughts ppl commonly have are…

  • doing something more is practice and so you get better, and if you dont use it you lose it (**in**creased willingful engagement -> **in**creased mental capacity; **de**creased willingful engagement -> **de**creased mental capacity)
  • doing something less often (in moderation) gives your mind a break so that you wont be burnt out, each time it will be like a treat that you will be more receptive to because you havent done it in a while (**de**creased willingful engagement -> **in**creased mental capacity; **in**creased willigful engagement -> **de**creased mental capacity)

so these two lines of thought are very commonly thought but they are also very much at odds XD they cant both be true all the time!! but i think the real answer is that both doing something more and doing something less can improve your brain’s capacity and receptivity, and that its just about timing!!! as with most things, theres a cycle of being busy getting tired, resting and getting back to it engaging with the thing at the right time means you will be most receptive to it, enjoy it the most, and improve your capacity the most engaging at the wrong time and you wont be functionig well, you wont like it, and you wont get nearly as much “good practice” out of it, you may even make backwards progress!!! jsut like over working aa muscle, over working ur brain isnt a good thing ! XD

so its like a swing, if you are pushing someone on the swing, if you want them to go higher and higher, you have to push the swing at the right time, right when its at the apex of the swing in front of you about to fall away, if you push it right then, it goes higher than it did the last time, but if you push it at the wrong time (like when its falling back towards you) you actually hinder it!

pleasure in something and mental capacity go hand in hand, when you are excited about something, you are rapidly absorbing new information and forming new memories !! :0 so the way that u find the right timing to engage in an activity is ideally to listen to ur brain !! just like u listen to ur muscles for when they want to move and when they want to rest, u can listen to ur brain, when ur in the mood for something, that means ur brain is telling u, hey !!!! its the ideal time to engage in and grow these particular networks !!!

so this is what i think XD more isnt always better, and less isnt always better, more isnt always worse and less isnt always worse, things r just best when its in sync with ur brains natural cycles of organizing its networks !! :0 and this is why ideals like brute discipline following strict schedules and SCHOOL are so awful to me XD because they dont account for this neurological NEED for flexibility !!! bc nobodies brains follow the same schedule !!!!!!!!!
