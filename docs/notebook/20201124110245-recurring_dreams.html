Midnight burglars

I have a lot of dreams where its midnight and there’s the imminent threat of burglars creeping outside and I have to stay on the lookout and be sneaky. Sometimes they have guns!

Disgusting public bathroom labyrinth

I have a ton of dreams where I have to use the bathroom, but the only bathroom I have access to is an extremely large and dirty maze-like bathroom with pee-puddles and poo-stains everywhere. And it smells just awful! XD When I have these dreams it always means that I need to use the bathroom in real life. XP

I get my room back

This is the most recurring dream I’ve ever had! Ever since we got evicted and I’ve had to share a room with my dad in a tiny cramped uncomfortable house with loud barking dogs no privacy ane bathroom we all fight over and hardly any space, I’ve dreamt extremely regularly about getting my room back! Sometimes it’s room I’ve had in the past, sometimes it’s a room that is similar to one I’ve had in the past, and sometimes it’s a new room altogether. Clearly I need my room back ;o; !!

I scream at my family

I have dreams a lot where I scream at my family for all the perceived injustice I had to deal with growing up. (Note: I had a great childhood and have really great parents overall, but it seems that even great and privileged childhoods tend to suffer from some level of trauma. In any case I’ve never gotten over it and am relentessly indignant about such things. XD)
