Nancy chow

Recipe

  1. start rice in large pot (2 cups) (temperature setting = 3)
  2. start lentils in small pot (1 cup) (temperature setting = 4)
  3. cut sweet potatos while cooking
  4. when rice and lentils are finished, pour lentils into rice pot
  5. pour sweet potatos into large pot, fill with water and begin boiling (temperature setting = 8)
  6. begin more lentils in small pot (1 cup) (temperature setting = 4)
  7. cut habaneros and pour into main pot
  8. pour spoonfuls of spice (2 spoonfulls each) into main pot
  9. drain and pour in all canned ingredients to main pot
  10. drain potatos and mash up and mix into main pot
  11. pour lentils into main pot
  12. heat up any frozen ingredients in the small pot and pour into main pot when thawed
  13. pour two pots contents back and fourth a few times and mix them up good, and finally back into main pot
  14. scoop out my lunch for the day, and put rest into fridge
  15. pour the remaining bits that dont fit in pot into my 8oz container and freeze
  16. move 8oz container from freezer to fridge two days before using it
