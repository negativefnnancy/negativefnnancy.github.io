This is my daily agenda!! 0_0

It consists of the basic routine appended with slots for the sprint, the marathon, and the leisure time.

Whenever I find myself super hyper and energetic about a project, I’ll move it to the sprint section. XD Otherwise, if it’s something I want to ride out over a long period of time, I’ll make it a marathon. But I don’t want to have too many marathons at once, and I don’t want to have more than one sprint at once!

I’ll let myself spend as much time a day on the marathons as I feel up to, as long as I do, say, a single pomodoro’s worth. On days when I have no sprints, I’ll spend plenty of time on the marathons.
