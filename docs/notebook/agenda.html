This is my daily agenda!! 0_0

Okay so this is my basic routine! On top of this, I’ll also spend time working towards goals. A goal is something that is a clearly defined accomplishment that I can clock my time towards in and out. When I need a boost in focus, I’ll use pomodoros!

My time spent working towards a goal can be either a sprint or a marathon. Whether I treat it as a sprint or a marathon is dependant on my learn drive, which is variable from day to day, so I’ll not expclitly record any distinctions here.

About lunch! I like to meal prep about a week’s worth in advance. I used to plan to do this every Sunday, but I’m trying to be a little more flexible because the real world doesn’t always sync up exactly how I would like it to. XD So I’ll say that I will just cook on the days that I run out of food! (This also implies going to the store on those days, or the day before, in preparation.)

Right before I go on walks I always cook my dinner (oatmeal) and leave it on my table with the lid over it so that it cools but also stays relatively warm for me by the time I get back inside from the walk.

My plan is to never eat before noon (so that it doesn’t take away from my precious morning energy) and to never eat after 7pm (so it doesn’t affect my sleep).

Leisure time means I get comfy in bed and do whatever I want to relax until bedtime!
